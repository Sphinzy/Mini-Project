Ikos Dassia is a luxury 5-star resort located on the beautiful east coast of Corfu, Greece. Opened in 2018, it offers a combination of modern architecture, lush green landscapes, and pristine private beaches. The resort features elegant rooms and suites, multiple restaurants with Michelin-star inspired menus, several pools, and extensive leisure activities for families and couples alike.

The resort offers over 400 stylish rooms and suites, some with private pools and direct beach access. Facilities include 7 swimming pools, a spa with wellness treatments, fitness center, tennis courts, kids clubs, and a private 600-meter sandy beach awarded the Blue Flag for cleanliness. Guests also enjoy free access to activities like yoga, paddleboarding, and cultural excursions.

Ikos Dassia features 7 à la carte restaurants offering diverse cuisine styles including Greek, Italian, Asian, and Mediterranean, many overseen by Michelin-star chefs. The resort also runs a unique "Dine Out" program allowing guests to dine at selected local restaurants at no extra charge. Evening entertainment includes live music, theatrical shows, and a nightclub, providing a lively atmosphere throughout the week.
