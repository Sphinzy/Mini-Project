About

Nido, Mar-Bella Collection is a luxurious adults-only resort located on the beautiful island of Corfu, Greece. Nestled along a stunning stretch of coastline, the resort offers breathtaking sea views, elegant suites with private pools or whirlpools, and a serene atmosphere perfect for relaxation. Known for its exceptional service and refined Greek hospitality, Nido combines modern comforts with traditional charm. Guests can enjoy gourmet dining, an infinity pool overlooking the Ionian Sea, a spa, and direct access to the beach. Its tranquil setting and sophisticated design make it an ideal destination for couples seeking a romantic getaway.
