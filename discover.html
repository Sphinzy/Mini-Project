Travelers' Choice Awards Best of the Best 2025

Every year, we award travelers' favorite destinations, hotels, restaurants, and things to do around the world, based on reviews and ratings collected over 12 months. The 2025 winners are a celebration of everything we did last year—all the places we discovered and all the times we just said "yes" to new adventures.
